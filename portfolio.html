Featured Case Study

Smart Irrigation System for Rice Fields in Punjab

Project Overview

Implemented a comprehensive smart irrigation system for 500 hectares of rice fields in Punjab, helping farmers optimize water usage while maintaining optimal crop growth.

Challenges

  • Declining groundwater levels in the region
  • Inconsistent rainfall patterns
  • High water requirements for rice cultivation
  • Limited technical knowledge among farmers

Our Solution

We deployed a network of soil moisture sensors across the fields, connected to our cloud-based analytics platform. The system provided real-time data on soil moisture levels and automatically adjusted irrigation schedules based on crop needs and weather forecasts.
