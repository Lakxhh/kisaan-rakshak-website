About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind Kisaan Rakshak

Our Story

Kisaan Rakshak was founded in 2018 with a simple yet powerful mission: to empower farmers with technology and data-driven insights to improve agricultural productivity and sustainability.

What started as a small team of agricultural engineers and software developers has grown into a comprehensive agricultural technology company serving thousands of farmers across India.

Our journey began when we witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by farmers in optimizing water usage and protecting crops from diseases. We realized that by combining IoT sensors, satellite imagery, and artificial intelligence, we could provide farmers with actionable insights to make better decisions.

Today, Kisaan Rakshak is at the forefront of agricultural innovation, continuously developing new solutions to address the evolving needs of modern farming.

Our Mission

To transform agriculture through innovative technology solutions that enable farmers to optimize resources, increase yields, and practice sustainable farming.

Our Vision

A world where every farmer has access to advanced agricultural technology, enabling efficient, profitable, and environmentally sustainable farming practices.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Continuously developing new solutions
  • Sustainability: Promoting environmentally friendly practices
  • Empowerment: Enabling farmers with knowledge and tools
  • Integrity: Operating with honesty and transparency

Our Founder's Story

The inspiration behind Kisaan Rakshak

Laksh Choudhary

Founder & CEO

Kisaan Rakshak was born from personal experience. Growing up in a farming family in Rajasthan, Laksh Choudhary witnessed firsthand the challenges that farmers face daily. His family struggled with unpredictable weather patterns, water scarcity, and crop diseases that significantly impacted their livelihood.

Determined to find solutions, Laksh began researching agricultural technology and speaking with thousands of farmers across the region. He discovered that many were facing the same problems - inefficient irrigation systems, lack of timely information about weather and soil conditions, and limited access to modern farming techniques.

After surveying over 1,000 farmers, Laksh confirmed that these challenges were widespread and decided to create a solution that would be accessible to all farmers, regardless of the size of their operations. This led to the founding of Kisaan Rakshak, a platform that combines traditional farming knowledge with modern technology to create sustainable, efficient agricultural practices.

Today, Laksh leads Kisaan Rakshak with a deep understanding of farmers' needs and a commitment to developing practical, affordable solutions that make a real difference in their lives.
