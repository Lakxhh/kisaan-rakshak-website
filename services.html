Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to set up the system?

The initial setup typically takes 1-2 weeks, depending on the size of your farm and the services you choose. Our team will handle the installation of sensors and provide training on using the platform.

Do I need internet connectivity on my farm?

Our sensors use low-power, long-range communication technology that works in most rural areas. For optimal performance, basic internet connectivity is recommended but not required for all features.

How accurate are the weather predictions?

Our weather predictions combine data from multiple sources and have an accuracy rate of over 85% for 3-day forecasts. We continuously improve our models based on local conditions and historical data.

Can I integrate with my existing irrigation system?

Yes, our system is designed to integrate with most modern irrigation systems. Our team will assess your current setup and provide the necessary adapters or controllers for seamless integration.

What crops do you support?

We support a wide range of crops including wheat, rice, cotton, sugarcane, vegetables, and fruits. Our system is adaptable to different crop types and growing conditions.

Is training provided for using the platform?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for all users. This includes hands-on sessions, video tutorials, and documentation. Our support team is also available to answer any questions you may have.
